About TOOLong

TOOLong is a rental marketplace platform that allows students and communities to rent school materials and equipment instead of purchasing them. It promotes accessibility, affordability, and sustainability.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To become a trusted and accessible community equipment rental platform.

Mission: Connect tool owners and renters through a safe, convenient, and reliable digital platform.
